## Matthew Harris
**b. 7 November 1834 | Hucknall Torkard, Nottinghamshire, England | d. 6 March 1908 | Hilliard, Uinta, Wyoming, United States**

Matthew Harris
Was ordained an Elder in the Hilliard Ward, LDS Church in October 1899 by John Crawford.

Matthew died of general debility.

=== Life Sketch ===
When Matthew Harris was born on November 7, 1834, in Hucknall, Nottinghamshire, England, his father, Mark, was 33 and his mother, Esther, was 30. He married Sarah Ann Widdowson Goddard on July 22, 1867. His family were converts to The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints.  He, Sarah and their children came to America in 1885 and settled in Alma, Wyoming. They had a total of ten children.  He was employed by the Union Pacific Railway Company for a period of twenty-seven years and during his later life he engaged successfully in stock raising and farming in Uinta County.  He died on March 6, 1908, in Hilliard, Wyoming, at the age of 73, and was buried in Evanston, Wyoming.

She married Matthew Harris on July 22, 1867.   She was the mother of ten children.  She had  2 children previous to her marriage to Matthew Harris. She died July 31, 1914 in Hilliard, Wyoming and was buried in Evanston, Wyoming.
