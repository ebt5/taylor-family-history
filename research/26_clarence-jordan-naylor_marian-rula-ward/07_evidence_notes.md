# Evidence Notes

## Confirmed
- Clarence Jordan Naylor was born 14 Aug 1927 in Safford, Arizona.
- He died 25 May 1980 in San Diego and was buried at Greenwood Cemetery.
- He married Marian Ward.
- He served as a missionary in the Eastern States.
- He served as bishop, bishop's counselor, and was installed as president of the San Diego North Stake in 1970.
- He worked as an FBI special agent.
- The family was publicly recognized as a San Diego Family of the Year household.

## Probable
- Marian Rula Ward is Erik's maternal grandmother and the "Marian Ward of Los Angeles" named in the 1970 article.
- The Family of the Year article dates from around 1969-1970 based on the church clipping and children's ages.

## Disputed / unresolved
- exact marriage date/place
- exact number of biological vs adopted children across the whole family timeline versus article snapshot
- precise duration of Jordan's FBI career (20 years vs more than 25 years)
- Marian's independent life details remain thin in the available source set
